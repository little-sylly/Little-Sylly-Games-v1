      How to Play

      Secret Signals

      Step 1 — The Vault 🔐

      Each team gets 4 secret keywords.

      Memorise them! They're numbered 1–4 and only your team can see them.

      Step 2 — Encrypt 📡

      Each round, one player gets a secret code.

      The code is 3 numbers (e.g. 3-1-4). Give one clue for each number — cryptic enough that the other team can't guess it, clear enough that your own team can!

      Step 3 — Intercept 🕵️

      The opposing team tries to crack your code.

      They see your clues and all previous clues — patterns emerge over time! If they guess correctly: they score an Interception.

      Step 4 — Decode 🔑

      Your own team must also decode the code.

      If your own team gets it wrong — that's a Misfire. You're talking in riddles no one understands!

      Winning 🏆

      Win by intercepting the enemy's transmissions.
      Lose by misfiring twice — your own team can't understand you!

      How to Play

      Great Minds

      Step 1 — The Setup 🎯

      Two players. One phone.

      You're each shown the same pair of words. Your job: think of one word that connects them both.

      Step 2 — The Rules 🚫

      Don't look at each other's answers.

      Each player enters their word privately — no peeking! You also can't use any part of either starting word.

      Step 3 — The Meld 🤝

      After the 3-2-1 countdown, both answers are revealed. If they match — Mind Meld! If not, your two words become the new starting pair. Keep going until you meld!

      Lower rounds = bigger brains. 🧠

      How to Play

      Just Enough Cooks

      Step 1 — Get the Order 🍽️

      Everyone sees the same secret food.

      A food item — like "Pizza" or "Nachos" — appears on screen. Everyone reads it, then passes the phone.

      Step 2 — Prep your Ingredients 🧑‍🍳

      Each player secretly enters 3 words.

      What ingredients would you use? Think carefully — you want to match others without being too obvious.

      Step 3 — Hit the Sweet Spot 🌟

      Fewer matches = more points.

      A 2-player match scores the most. The more Chefs share a word, the fewer points it's worth — until it tips into Spoilt (Too Many Cooks!). Unique words are Rotten. With penalties on: Rotten costs −10 pts, and Spoilt costs −2 pts per Chef who picked it.

      Step 4 — Win the Shift 🏆

      Most Golden ingredients across all dishes wins. Five-star effort right there!
